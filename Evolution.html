Evolution:

Evolution of the internet refers to a transformation that took place that changed the way we communicate and/or access information. The standard of the internet changed to TCP on the birth of the internet on January 1, 1983. Important dates for the evolution of the internet:

Content about evolution:

Year Information
1960 The ARPANET (Advanced Research Projects Agency Network) was developed. The ideas behind a computer network was that it could withhold nuclear attacks. This computer network become the first successful one, which ended up in universities.
1970 The introduction of the IP (Internet Protocol) / TCP (Transmission Control Protocol) were developed for data transmission. This protocol allows communication between different networks.
1990 The WWW (World Wide Web) and the Internet were introduced by Tim Berners-Lee. The concepts that were also introduced are for example hypertext. The .com growth in online businesses increase astronautically.
2000 The rise of Social Media platforms were developed. Online social media platforms like Facebook transformed online social interactions. The way people consumed entertainment after online steaming were developed changed a lot.
2010 The mobile internet and cloud computing were developed. Cloud computing were developed by J.C.R Licklider in 1960 and finally introduced publicly available in 2010. Mobile internet were developed by Martin Cooper and Eric Tigerstedt. Cloud computing is used for the store of data and application accessibility.
2023 The internet of everything and beyond. The internet keeps growing every day. No wonder 100 million more or less people uses chat GPT regularly. There have been profound impacts on our modern society lives. Like with communication, entertainment and education.
