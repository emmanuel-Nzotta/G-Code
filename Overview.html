WWW vs the Internet:

World Wide Web referred to as the web. It is a subset of the internet. The World Wide Web was invented by Tim Berners – Lee in 1989, he was working at CERN at that time. CERN is known as a European Organization for Nuclear Research. His goal was a system that scientists could use around the world to access and share information easily and that would be available to do so at any time, day, or night. The developed the protocols and technologies to make this goal work. The web allowed hypertext which users used to navigate between the documents.
